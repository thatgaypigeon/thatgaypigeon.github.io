Lexend is an open source sans-serif typeface designed by Bonnie Shaver-Troup and Thomas Jockin, intended to reduce visual stress and improve reading performance.
Atkinson Hyperlegible is a freely available typeface developed by the Braille Institute of America intended to optimise legibility for the visually impaired.
Noto Sans is a free and open source humanist sans-serif typeface designed by Google to cover all the world's writing systems defined in the Unicode Standard.
Open Dyslexic is a free and open source typeface designed by Abelardo González to help mitigate some of the common reading struggles faced by people with dyslexia.
